# HR Recruitment Analysis

## Project Introduction

Recruitment analytics helps organizations understand hiring demand, applicant behavior, workforce trends, and recruitment effectiveness. This project analyzes job posting data to uncover insights related to talent demand, hiring activity, company attractiveness, industry growth, and geographic recruitment patterns.

Using Microsoft Excel, the project transforms raw recruitment data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI reporting, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze recruitment and job posting data to:

* Identify high-demand job roles.
* Analyze hiring activity across companies.
* Understand applicant behavior and competition.
* Evaluate industry-wise hiring trends.
* Examine geographic hiring patterns.
* Support data-driven recruitment and workforce planning decisions.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains job posting information across multiple companies, industries, and locations, including:

* Job Role and Designation
* Company Information
* Company Size
* Company Followers
* Industry
* Work Type (Remote/Onsite)
* Employment Type
* Applicant Count
* Experience Level
* City and State Information
* Job Descriptions

This dataset simulates a real-world HR analytics scenario where organizations seek to optimize recruitment strategies and understand talent demand patterns.

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Job Role Demand Insight

Some job roles received significantly higher hiring demand and applicant interest, indicating strong market demand and high competition for those positions.

---

### 2. Company Hiring Insight

Top companies showed higher recruitment activity and attracted a large number of applicants, highlighting strong employer branding and hiring expansion.

---

### 3. Work Type Insight

Remote and hybrid work opportunities attracted higher applicant engagement, showing growing preference for flexible work environments.

---

### 4. Industry Insight

The IT and technology-related industries showed the highest hiring demand, indicating continuous growth and talent requirements in the technology sector.

---

### 5. Geographic Insight

Major metropolitan cities showed stronger hiring activity and applicant concentration, highlighting regional employment hubs and talent availability.

---

### 6. Applicant Trend Insight

Job postings from highly recognized companies and industries attracted more applicants compared to smaller organizations.

---

## Recommendations

* Focus recruitment efforts on high-demand talent segments.
* Strengthen employer branding to improve applicant attraction.
* Expand hiring strategies within high-growth industries.
* Utilize geographic insights for workforce planning.
* Promote flexible work arrangements to improve candidate engagement.
