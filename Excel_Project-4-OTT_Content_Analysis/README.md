# OTT Content Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

Over-the-Top (OTT) platforms depend on data-driven content strategies to attract viewers, improve engagement, and optimize content investments. This project focuses on analyzing an OTT content catalog using Microsoft Excel to uncover trends related to content distribution, genre performance, audience targeting, geographic contribution, and portfolio diversification.

The project demonstrates the application of data cleaning, exploratory analysis, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI reporting, and interactive dashboard development to generate actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to evaluate the OTT platform's content portfolio and identify opportunities for improving content strategy through data analysis.

Key objectives include:

* Analyzing the distribution of Movies and TV Shows.
* Evaluating content growth trends over time.
* Identifying dominant genres and content categories.
* Assessing country-wise content contribution.
* Understanding audience targeting through rating analysis.
* Measuring content freshness and portfolio balance.
* Generating strategic recommendations for content expansion and diversification.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of **8,809 content records** and includes the following attributes:

* Title
* Content Type (Movie / TV Show)
* Director
* Cast
* Country
* Date Added
* Release Year
* Rating
* Duration
* Genre Categories

The dataset represents a diverse content library and provides a realistic scenario for content portfolio analysis and business decision-making.

## Key Business Insights

### Content Growth Insight

The platform has demonstrated strong content expansion over recent years, indicating an active content acquisition and library growth strategy.

### Genre Insight

Drama and International content categories dominate the catalog, reflecting strong audience demand while highlighting potential concentration risks.

### Geographic Insight

Content production is concentrated within a limited number of countries, suggesting opportunities to diversify regional content investments.

### Audience Strategy Insight

The platform maintains a broad range of content ratings, enabling effective audience segmentation across different age groups and viewer preferences.

### Engagement Insight

TV Shows frequently contain multiple seasons, supporting binge-watching behavior and increasing long-term viewer engagement.

### Portfolio Insight

While the content library offers considerable variety, opportunities remain to improve balance across genres, regions, and audience segments.

## Executive Summary

### Key Strengths

* Strong focus on recently released content.
* Balanced mix of Movies and TV Shows.
* Broad genre coverage supporting diverse audience interests.
* Consistent content growth over time.

### Key Risks

* High dependency on a limited number of countries.
* Concentration in specific genres.
* Uneven distribution across audience categories.

### Strategic Recommendations

1. Increase investment in underrepresented genres to improve portfolio diversity.
2. Expand content acquisition from emerging and international markets.
3. Maintain a balanced mix of legacy and newly released content.
4. Diversify audience-targeted content categories to broaden viewer reach.
